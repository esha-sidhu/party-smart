Preparation

The day before

- Ensure your tickets are valid
- Make sure that you everyone is hydrated
- Make sure everyone eats a good amount of food
- Coordinate with your group! Make sure to have a plan

At the event

- Spot out areas with medical personnel, emergency staff
- Have a plan in case of emergency
- Be honest with your group about what you intend to do at the event
- Have a designated person to watch over people if necessary
- If allowed in the venue, narcan can save lives'
