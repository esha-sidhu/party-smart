FAQ

Q)What is the purpose of this app?
A)The purpose of this app is to help recognize early symptoms and prevent possibly life threatening scenarios for people.

Q)Do I need to be signed in for access to any of the resources on this app?
A) No, all resources will be accessible even without having an account created.

Q) Will my medical information be displayed for everyone to see?
A) No, only people in your group will see your medical information.

Q) Am I able to join multiple groups at the same time?
A) No, currently you are only able to be in one group at a time. When you create a new group the previous will be deleted.
